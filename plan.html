CossiePlay aims to be more successful by increasing its sales revenue and amount of customers. Thus, this would be achievable by providing fun, promotions and an enjoyable environment for cosplay lovers.

The success of the website will be seen through an increase in people cosplaying at CossiePlay and in an increase of sales revenue.  If the sales and cosplayers at CossiePlay increased significantly, this will mark that the website is successful.

Currently, we mostly have cosplayers from the age of 35 – 50 years old. However, now we are also targeting at a younger audience such as high school kids and younger adults to participate in cosplaying.
